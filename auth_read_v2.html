Complete Online Privacy Guide 2026

This guide covers everything you need to know about protecting your privacy online.

Step 1: Check Your Email Security

Your email account is the gateway to all your other accounts. Make sure you have 2FA enabled and review your recent login activity.

Step 2: Review Browser Privacy Settings

Check your browser settings to ensure maximum privacy protection.

Step 3: Audit Your Google Account

Review your Google account security dashboard to see connected apps, recent activity, and recovery options.
